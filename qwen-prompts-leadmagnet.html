01
Invoice Data Extraction
"Extract all fields from this invoice image: vendor name, invoice number, date, line items with quantities and unit prices, subtotal, tax, and total. Return as structured JSON."
Use for: Automating accounts payable, removing manual data entry from finance teams.
02
Contract Clause Identification
"Review this contract image and identify: payment terms, termination clauses, liability caps, renewal conditions, and any non-standard clauses that require legal review. Flag anything unusual."
Use for: Pre-screening contracts before they reach legal counsel, saving review time.
03
Receipt Categorisation
"Analyse this receipt image. Extract: merchant name, date, total amount, payment method, and categorise the expense type (travel, meals, equipment, software, other). Flag if over $100."
Use for: Automating expense report processing and policy compliance checks.
04
Multi-Document Comparison
"Compare these two document images side by side. List every difference in terms, values, dates, and conditions. Highlight any changes that could have financial or legal impact."
Use for: Contract redlining, proposal version control, policy change tracking.

05
Dashboard Screenshot to Data
"Read this dashboard screenshot and extract all visible metrics, their values, time periods, and trends. Return as a structured table I can paste into a spreadsheet."
Use for: Pulling data from tools that have no API export, or when you only have a screenshot.
06
Chart Data Recovery
"Analyse this chart image and reconstruct the underlying data. Estimate the values for each data point, label the axes, identify the trend, and summarise the key insight in one sentence."
Use for: Recovering data from old reports, competitor screenshots, or presentations without source files.
07
Handwritten Notes Transcription
"Transcribe all text from this handwritten notes image. Preserve the structure including headings, bullet points, and action items. Flag any words that are unclear with [unclear]."

08
Spreadsheet Screenshot Parser
"Convert this spreadsheet screenshot into a CSV-formatted text output. Preserve all column headers, row labels, and cell values. Note any merged cells or formatting that carries meaning."
Use for: Extracting data from emailed spreadsheet screenshots or locked PDF tables.

Competitor Product Analysis
"Analyse this competitor product image. Identify visible features, quality indicators, likely price positioning, and what customer pain point it addresses. Suggest one gap our product could exploit."

Packaging Compliance Check
"Review this product packaging image. Check for: required regulatory labels, ingredient or material disclosures, safety warnings, contact information, and barcode visibility. List anything missing or unclear."

Site or Facility Inspection
"Analyse this site photo. Identify any visible safety hazards, maintenance issues, or compliance concerns. Rate severity (low/medium/high) and suggest immediate action for anything rated medium or above."
